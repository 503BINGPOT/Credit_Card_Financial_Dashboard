# Credit_Card_Financial_Dashboard

**Overview**

Built a comprehensive Credit Card Financial Dashboard in Power BI to deliver real-time insights into customer spending behavior, transaction trends, revenue generation, and overall credit card business performance. The solution enables stakeholders to monitor key financial KPIs and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

**Key Features**

* Developed dynamic dashboards to analyze revenue, interest earnings, transaction volume, customer acquisition, and card usage patterns.
* Created customer segmentation views based on demographics such as age, income group, occupation, education, and marital status.
* Implemented drill-through reports, filters, and slicers for detailed exploration of financial and customer metrics.
* Designed weekly performance tracking dashboards to identify trends, growth opportunities, and operational bottlenecks.

**Data Processing & Analytics**

* Utilized SQL for data extraction and preparation from relational datasets.
* Performed data cleaning, transformation, and modeling using Power Query.
* Created advanced DAX measures and calculated columns to derive meaningful business KPIs and financial metrics.
* Built a scalable data model with optimized relationships to support efficient reporting and analysis.

**Tech Stack**

Power BI, SQL, Power Query, DAX, Data Modeling, Business Intelligence, Data Visualization, Financial Analytics

**Business Impact**

Provided a centralized analytics solution that transforms raw credit card transaction data into actionable insights, enabling improved monitoring of customer behavior, revenue performance, and key business trends through intuitive and interactive dashboards.
